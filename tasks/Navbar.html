Nav bar

With Bootstrap, a navigation bar can extend or collapse, depending on the screen size. A standard navigation bar is created with the .navbar class, followed by a responsive collapsing class: .navbar-expand-xxl|xl|lg|md|sm (stacks the navbar vertically on xxlarge, extra large, large, medium or small screens).
